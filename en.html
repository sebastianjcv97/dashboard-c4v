The company at a glance

C4V Láser sells CO2 laser cutting machines to entrepreneurs in Peru, Ecuador, Bolivia and across the region. It imports directly from China (RUIDA), provides lifetime training (C4V School) and offers technical support in Spanish. How it makes money

It sells laser machines (high ticket) + spare parts/service. The engine is generating leads on social media, converting them into sales, and supporting the customer with training and support so they buy again and refer others.

Technology stack

Odoo 19 Enterprise (ERP/CRM, erp.c4vlaser.com), ManyChat (WA/IG lead capture bot), WordPress + Tutor LMS (C4V School), n8n + Railway (automations), WhatsApp (internal communication and support).

The big pain today

Leads are lost due to manual follow-up, and support has no coordinator (the engineers pass the buck and the customer complains). That is where technology can move the needle right now.

The group of companies

C4V operates as a group: one commercial brand with several legal entities per country and the purchasing hub in China. Separately there are the projects and lines (the academy, the coaching) that are not separate companies. Everything is managed from a single Odoo (DB c4v_migrado).

AI products and platforms

CeVi · web voice demo in progress

AI voice assistant (CeVi) that holds a conversation, answers laser support questions and creates tickets. A public website to test the concept before bringing it into the machine.
Stack: web on GitHub Pages + FastAPI backend on Railway + Claude Haiku + Edge TTS voice. Integrated with Odoo (creates tickets and contacts) and identifies the customer by order number. → sebastianjcv97.github.io/cevi-demo

CeVi · embedded in machines (Ruida) doc ready

The same voice AI, but inside each laser machine (wake word "Hola CeVi"). It is the commercial differentiator for 2026/2027.
Status: requirements document finished in ES/EN/ZH, ready to hand over to Ruida so they assign an engineer. ESP32 hardware + AI in the cloud. ~200 machines.

Toby · banking anti-fraud in development

Verifies that the customer's transfer has actually cleared in the bank before shipping the machine. A bounced payment = a lost machine.
How: OCR of the voucher + reading the bank portal (real Chrome via CDP), on a dedicated Mac. Pending: completing the bank logins and account data to go into production.
Internal system and automation

C4V multi-agent system operational

A "virtual office" of specialized bots that automate tasks: Michael leads; Oscar (Odoo), Kelly (ManyChat), Jim (social media/competition), Ryan (marketing), Dwight (auditing), Creed (news/exchange rate), Pam (notifications), Andy (SEO), Toby (banking), Mei (China).

Strategy — where it is heading

Mission, vision, values and the strategy map (Balanced Scorecard). This gives the director direction: not just how C4V operates, but what it aims to achieve.

🎯 Mission

To provide high-quality, reliable and accessible laser cutting solutions that make it easier to create and consolidate businesses across diverse sectors. We support with technical assistance, constant training and an ecosystem of tools that adds value at every stage of the venture.

🌍 Vision

To be the leading brand in laser cutting machines in Peru and Latin America, recognized globally for its innovation, efficiency and commitment to entrepreneurs; with a strong community that identifies us as the best laser machine in Peru and their strategic ally.

Values

1 · Commitment to the entrepreneur

We support from the first cut: training, support and tools so the business grows.

2 · Technological transformation

Solutions like Cero Quemado and the Pro IA line (operate the machine from your phone).

3 · Professionalism

Seriousness, efficiency, clear warranties and after-sales service.

4 · Entrepreneurial solidarity

We share knowledge, templates and strategies. If you grow, we grow too.

5 · Transparency

We communicate products, benefits and conditions honestly.

6 · Optimism and motivation

We believe in the potential of every entrepreneur and inspire them.

7 · Responsibility with impact

Sustainable businesses, social initiatives and care for the environment.

8 · Collaborative work

A support community before, during and after the sale.

9 · Respect

Consideration, courtesy and empathy; we value diversity.

10 · Passion for teaching

We teach with patience so anyone can master laser cutting.
